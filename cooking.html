Youtube


Youtube is an amazing source of information. This includes cooking tutorials. Some of the Youtubers I follow are "Made With Lau" and "ThatDudeCanCook". In fact, I learnt got my carbonara recipe from ThatDudeCanCook. Check out the recipe in the video on the left.



Food Science classes


Taking cooking classes would expose you to a wide variety of experiences. One such module I've taken would be the Everyday Science in Food module in NIE. In this module, I was given the opportunity to learn food science and further ignited my interest in cooking

Cooking Workshops


If modules that span an entire semester are not suitable, short term workshops are also available. These courses are a break from the hectic lifestyle and helped me to ease my mind at the end of the day. The picture on the left shows a carbonara I made during one of the cooking workshops I went to.
