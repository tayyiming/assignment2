Spark of Interest

Thinking back, what started my interest in cooking was some simple video clips I saw on youtube and scenes depicting food in movies. The mouth-watering shots of food cooked and plated by professional gave me hope that i could replicate dishes like those for my family. Another reason which sparked my interest was the tasty dishes that my mother would cook for the family.

Start of the journey

However, all these remained as an interest until army. Towards the end of my national service, I started to have some free time to myself. During which, I started learning from my mother and spent time to test out some dishes. One of the very first dishes I cooked was a simple stir-fried franks with potato, a typical dish sold at economic rice stalls.

Moving Forward

As time progresses and more Youtube videos caught my attention, I start learning dishes that I like. One such example was the carbonara.

Trying Out

Sometimes, I would test out my culinary skills and tried more complex dishes like the chicken lollipop. The hard part of this dish was to prepare the lollipops which took me a good 5 minutes per piece.
