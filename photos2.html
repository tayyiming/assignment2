RedMan by Phoon Huat Sells a wide range of baking supplies and other cooking ingredients. This ranges from baking tools and accessories to meat and seafood. Most stores open at 10am, with the exception of a few. Closing hours vary from 7:30pm to 10pm, depending on the store. Visit the link to view the opening hours of the various stores. The Cheese Shop Sells a wide assortment of cheeses, as well as other products such as alcohol, biscuits and dried fruits. Typically opens from 11am to 7pm. Though timing may vary a little depending on the stores. Stores at Upper Bukit Timah and Upper Thomson does not open on Mondays
Scoop Wholefoods Sells premium wholefoods from across the globe, including tea, nuts, grains, snacks, granola, freshly churned nut butter, salt and more. They also have a mission to have zero-waste. 10am-9pm. With slight variation between stores. Visit the link to view the opening hours of the various stores.
